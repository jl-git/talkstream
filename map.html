The seminar map

The map page shows all seminars by their hosting universities' location on a Google map. Each university that is currently hosting one or more seminars will be represented by a doctor's cap . Clicking one of the doctor's cap will show a information box that contains the university's name and a list of its on going seminars along with their times. The total number of seminars of a university will be shown after the university's name.
